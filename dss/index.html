OVERVIEW

Assuming no prior knowledge of statistics or coding and only minimal knowledge of math, Data Analysis for Social Science teaches the fundamentals of survey research, predictive models, and causal inference, plus how to analyze real-world data with the free and popular statistical program R.

It progresses by teaching how to solve one kind of problem after another, bringing in methods as needed. It flips the script of traditional statistics textbooks, engaging students by demonstrating from the very beginning how data analysis can answer interesting questions.

FEATURES / HIGHLIGHTS

  • Step-by-step guide to analyzing real-world data using R.
  • Designed to accommodate students with varying math backgrounds.
  • Includes cheatsheets, tips, recalls, and interactive graphs.
